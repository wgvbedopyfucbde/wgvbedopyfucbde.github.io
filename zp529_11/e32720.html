Singlet
A warm-weather sleeveless top that’s sleek, lightweight, and made for hard summer running.

Feeling light and running free the Singlet is purpose-built to embrace the joy of warm-weather running in all its stripped-back glory. This sleeveless top is made with a lightweight and stretchy Italian mesh fabric, offering maximum breathability with minimum weight.

The cut is loose but not baggy perfect for purposeful training or working hard in warm weather while the unique construction method sees all seams moved away from high-motion areas to prevent rubbing. A dropped, rounded neckline adds further to on-body comfort when running, and laser cut hems keep the grams down even further.
